The Key Finding

Every product page ends in “Enquire Now,” not checkout.

Every sale today closes by hand over WhatsApp or email. That's fine for a boutique catalogue but it's why the Meta Ads in this plan run on Messages/Leads objectives instead of Purchases (Lot 06), and why fixing it is Move 01 in Lot 04.

Lot 02 The Market Right Now

A Steady, Growing Category

The global clutch market is growing from $6.3B to $8.2B by 2032 a steady 4.4% a year, inside a $78B handbag market where the premium tier is outgrowing the mass market. All of it favors a small, premium brand like Auraashè.

Premiumization

Shoppers are trading up premium is growing faster than mass market, and “affordable luxury” is exactly Auraashè's lane.

Social & Online Discovery

“Wedding guest clutch” and “crystal embellished clutch” are now real, searched shopping categories: free distribution for a brand that shows up well in-feed.

The Occasion Economy

Clutches sell against a calendar: weddings, prom, festive season, Diwali: so demand can be planned, not guessed at.

Lot 03: The Competitive Field

Four Tiers, One Open Gap

Mapping the field from heritage luxury to handmade sellers shows exactly where Auraashè can win.

Tier 1

The Heritage Anchor

Judith Leiber

Pioneered the crystal minaudière in 1963: pieces now sit in the Met, priced from $695+. Auraashè borrows the look at a fraction of the price.

Tier 2

Premium Contemporary

Gedebe · Karen Millen

Gedebe (~$945) and Karen Millen ($130–$190) show what “premium but reachable” looks like: Karen Millen's range nearly brackets Auraashè's own.

Tier 3

The Niche DTC Operator

SHE Clutches

Built ~89K followers around one category, but its content is mostly product shots and reviews: light on styling or founder story.

Tier 4

The Fragmented Field

Etsy & Shopify sellers

Hundreds of sellers prove real demand, but inconsistent branding and no founder face leave the door open.

No brand owns “beautifully photographed, story-led, $40–$135 crystal clutch” in the U.S.: that's the gap this plan is built to claim.
